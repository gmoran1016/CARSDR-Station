      RadioReference import needs the Pi to reach the internet. Two options:

      1. Import at home — connect the Pi to your home Wi-Fi (or temporarily to your phone's Personal Hotspot), do the import, then take it on the road. Frequencies are saved permanently.
      2. Temporary hotspot — in the car, enable your iPhone's Personal Hotspot, then on the Pi run:
        sudo raspi-config → System → Wireless LAN → connect to your hotspot. Import, then switch back.
